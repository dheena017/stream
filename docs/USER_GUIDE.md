# User Guide

Welcome to Antigravity AI, your intelligent multi-model AI companion. This guide will help you navigate the application and make the most of its features.

## Getting Started

### Login
When you first launch the application, you will be presented with a login screen.

**Default Credentials:**
- **Username:** `admin`
- **Password:** `admin123`

*Or*

- **Username:** `user`
- **Password:** `user123`

You can also register a new account by clicking the "Register" tab.

### Google Login
If configured by the administrator, you can also sign in using your Google account. Click the "Sign in with Google" button and follow the prompts.

## Dashboard
After logging in, you may land on the Dashboard (if configured) or directly in the Chat. The Dashboard provides an overview of your usage and quick access to features.

## Chat Interface
The core of the application is the Chat interface.

### 1. Selecting an AI Model
In the sidebar, you can select the "AI Provider" (e.g., OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Ollama) and the specific "Model" you wish to converse with.
*   **Tip:** Different models have different strengths. Experiment to find the best one for your task.

### 2. AI Brain Mode
Toggle "Enable AI Brain Mode" in the sidebar to combine multiple models.
-   **Learning Brain:** The AI learns from your interactions and improves over time.
-   **Thought Processing:** The AI may "think" before answering to provide more accurate responses.

### 3. Internet Search
Need up-to-date information?
-   Check **"Enable Internet Search"** in the sidebar.
-   The AI will search the web using DuckDuckGo to answer your questions with real-time data.
-   *See [Internet Search Guide](INTERNET_SEARCH_GUIDE.md) for more details.*

### 4. Voice Mode
You can talk to the AI!
-   Click the microphone icon (or "Voice Input" button) to start recording.
-   Speak your query, and the AI will transcribe it and respond.
-   If text-to-speech is enabled, the AI will speak the answer back to you.

### 5. Multimodal (Images & Video)
You can upload files to the chat:
-   **Images:** Upload images for analysis. The AI can describe them or answer questions about them.
    -   *Advanced Captioning:* Enable "Advanced Image Captioning (BLIP)" for more detailed descriptions (requires setup).
-   **Video/Audio:** Upload media files for transcription and analysis.

## Profile & Settings
Access your Profile from the sidebar to:
-   Update your personal information.
-   Change your password.
-   Manage preferences like Dark Mode.

## Tips
-   **Clear Chat:** Use the "Clear Chat" button in the sidebar to start a fresh conversation.
-   **Sidebar:** The sidebar contains all your configuration options. If it's closed, click the arrow in the top-left corner to open it.
