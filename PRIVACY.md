# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2024-05-23

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Stream AI Chat ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have control over your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 2. Data Collection
We collect the following types of information:
- **Account Information:** Username, email address (if provided), and password (hashed).
- **Chat History:** Messages you send and receive, including text, images, and files.
- **Preferences:** Settings such as theme, language, and model selection.
- **Usage Data:** Anonymous statistics about feature usage (e.g., number of messages sent, models used).

## 3. Data Usage
We use your data to:
- Provide and improve the chat service.
- Maintain your conversation history across sessions.
- personalize your experience.
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance performance and features.

**We do not sell your personal data to third parties.**

## 4. Data Storage and Security
- **Encryption:** Your chat messages are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (AES-128 via Fernet).
- **Local Storage:** Data is stored locally on the server hosting the application.
- **Security:** We employ reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

## 5. User Rights
You have the right to:
- **Access:** View your profile and export your data.
- **Rectification:** Update your profile information.
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data permanently.
- **Restriction:** Choose how long your chat history is retained.

## 6. Data Retention
By default, chat history is retained indefinitely to provide context for future conversations. You can change this in your Profile settings to auto-delete messages after 30, 60, or 90 days.

## 7. Third-Party Services
We utilize third-party AI models (e.g., OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic). When you interact with these models, your prompt content is sent to their respective APIs. Please review their privacy policies for more information on how they handle data.

## 8. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the administrator.
