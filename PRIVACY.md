# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to our AI Chat Application. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

## 2. Data We Collect
We collect the following information:
- **Account Information:** Username and encrypted password.
- **Chat History:** The content of your conversations with the AI.
- **Preferences:** Your settings, such as data retention periods.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
We use your data to:
- Provide and improve the chat service.
- Maintain your conversation history.
- Enforce your retention preferences.

## 4. Data Security
- **Encryption:** All chat messages are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (AES).
- **Storage:** Data is stored locally on the server.
- **Access:** Only authorized personnel have access to the server, but they cannot read your encrypted messages without the key.

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion
- **Retention:** By default, messages are retained for 30 days. You can adjust this in your settings.
- **Deletion:** You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. This action is irreversible.

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your data (via the Export feature).
- Modify your data (via settings).
- Delete your data.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at [support@example.com].
